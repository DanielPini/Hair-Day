About Hair Day

Who we are

Based in Madison, Wisconsin, Hair Day is a boutique salon specialising in cut, colour and styling. We also offer makeup and nail services. Our focus is on personalised care and technical skill delivered in a warm, relaxed environment.

Our approach

Every service begins with a consultation. Cuts are tailored to fit lifestyle and structure. Colour work is custom-formulated and executed with techniques that balance creativity and hair health. We are known for edgy colour and on-trend cuts, delivered with precision and attention to detail.

Treat yourself

Appointments at Hair Day are intended as time set aside for the client. Treatments, styling and grooming are performed to restore and refresh. We limit the number of colouring appointments each week to preserve quality; clients are advised to book well in advance.

Owner and founders

Pria, Hair Day’s owner, trained as a colourist and immediately specialised in custom, fashion-forward colouring techniques. She established the salon to create a “treat yourself” environment centred on excellent service. Pria opened Hair Day with her friend and stylist Garry.

Our team

The salon staff numbers seven: two stylists, three colourists, one manicurist and one make-up artist. Each team member works to a shared standard of craft and client care.

Keeping you inspired

To help clients maintain looks between visits, our stylists publish makeup and nail tutorials on the Hair Day website. The tutorials are practical, step-by-step guides designed for use at home.

Booking and availability

Because we accept a limited number of colour bookings each week, we recommend scheduling colour services several weeks in advance. For appointment requests and availability, please visit our booking page or contact the salon directly.
